About us

Who
are
we?

BUDDY & CO. was founded in 1948 by veterinerian and animal lover Dr. Herbert 'Buddy' Miller who wanted to provide affordable and quality food for all pets.

We are one of the first pet food companies to use scientific research and innovation to create formulas that meet the specific needs of different breeds, life stages, and health conditions.

Today, we a global leader in pet nutrition, with a wide range of brands and products for dogs and cats of all sizes and preferences. We are committed to delivering the highest quality, safety, and taste in every product we make.

We also support various animal welfare organizations and shelters through our Buddy Support program.
